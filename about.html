Citizen-powered air truth

Why BreathTruth is special

BreathTruth is built around a simple idea: the air people actually breathe on their streets may be very different from what distant monitoring points show.

Instead of only looking at official readings, BreathTruth gives space to lived experience, real symptoms, local observations, and community voices.

📍

Local, not distant

BreathTruth focuses on the roads, colonies, junctions, and industrial pockets where people actually live and travel.

👥

Powered by people

Citizens can report what they see and feel, from black smoke and unusual smell to burning eyes and breathing discomfort.

🌫️

Makes the invisible visible

It turns scattered local experiences into visible patterns, helping communities notice pollution hotspots more clearly.

This is not just air data.

This is lived reality.

BreathTruth stands out because it treats pollution not only as a number on a dashboard, but as something people physically experience in their daily lives.

Built for awareness. Built for community.

BreathTruth helps turn individual experiences into collective awareness, so pollution is not ignored just because it was measured somewhere else.
