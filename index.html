Why Official Monitors Miss Reality

Community reporting fills critical gaps in air quality monitoring

Limited Geographic Coverage

Official monitors are sparse and strategically placed. They miss hotspots in residential areas, busy roads, and industrial zones.

Delayed Data Release

Official reports are often delayed by hours or days. BreathTruth data is real-time, capturing incidents as they happen.

Human Experience Invisible

Numbers alone don't capture the real impact on health and daily life. Citizens' voices reveal what data cannot.
